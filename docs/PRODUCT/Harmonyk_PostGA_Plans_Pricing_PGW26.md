# Harmonyk — Post-GA Plans & Pricing (summary)

| Plan | Price (Monthly) | Best for | What you get | Key limits / notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | Solo founder kicking the tires | Workbench, Vault, Contracts/Decks/Accounts Builders (basic), Share Hub, basic Signatures, Contacts, Tasks/Calendar (core), Insights (basic); **Maestro** panel with tight usage; built-in Playbooks only; Google Drive + Gmail import. | Small Vault/storage caps; low signatures & AI/RAG/month; limited pack runs; **no Playbooks v2 editor**; short analytics history; community support. Viral CTAs enabled (recipient clone/keep-a-copy). |
| **Starter** | $30 / seat (≈$24 annual) | 1–3 founders running a raise + first customers | Everything in Free + higher caps (docs/storage/AI/signatures/packs); access to **core ~25 connectors** with a small number of active accounts; Contracts v2, Decks v2 (**Visual Assistant** rate-limited), Accounts v2; Contacts insights; Tasks/Calendar; **Playbooks v2 editor (limited)**; basic Deck Analytics & Deal views. | Moderate caps; fewer concurrent connectors; fewer Playbooks & runs/month; shorter data retention; simple branded digests; standard email support. **Free Collaborators** (unlimited) included. |
| **Pro** | $60 / seat (≈$48 annual) | 3–20 person startup making Harmonyk the default | Everything in Starter + much higher caps; most of the ~25 connectors concurrently (Drive/Gmail/Outlook/OneDrive/SharePoint/Dropbox/Box/Notion/Slack/Teams/QBO/Xero/Stripe/Asana/Linear/ClickUp/Trello/Jira/Slides/PowerPoint/Pitch/Canva); full **Playbooks v2** (more triggers/actions); full Deck Analytics + investor feedback loops; Deal/Investor dashboards; advanced funnels & admin usage dashboards; deeper Accounts→Decks integration. | High (not unlimited) caps; longer data retention; priority support; early-access feature flags. **Free Collaborators** (unlimited). |
| **Team** | $200 / 3 seats + $50/addl (≈20% annual) | Small funds or high-velocity pods | Everything in Pro; 3 seats included; top caps for docs/storage/AI/connectors/packs; granular admin controls (connectors, Playbooks, limits); roadmap feedback & feature flags; stronger security posture (DPA template, documented controls). | Top-end caps (pre-Enterprise); priority/concierge support; scope to negotiate assurances. **Free Collaborators** (unlimited) + branded Smart Share pages. |
